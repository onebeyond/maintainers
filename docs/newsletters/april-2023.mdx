# April's Newsletter (BETA)

## üì¢ Announcements and Updates

**üö® Important**
- [Socket.dev](https://socket.dev/) has been added to the maintainers' repository and other repositories. Here is an [example report]((https://github.com/onebeyond/maintainers/pull/68#issuecomment-1507269947)).
- As it is the 'Puente de Mayo', the next meeting will be held in one month on May 18th, 2023.
- May is [the Open Source maintainer Month](https://github.blog/2023-04-25-gearing-up-for-maintainer-month-this-may/)

**üì∞ News**
- [The Cybersecurity Handbook](https://github.com/guidesmiths/cybersecurity-handbook) will be transferred to the One Beyond Organization, and its content will be updated.
- We published the last release for [onebeyond/systemic-knex](https://github.com/onebeyond/systemic-knex) with the new provenance system in NPM. [See](https://twitter.com/kom_256/status/1648990100485726209)
- We are taking part in the [Scorecard StepSecurity Dashboard Beta](https://github.com/step-security/secure-repo/issues/2094#issuecomment-1513873406).
    - [Dashboard for Guidesmiths Org](https://app.stepsecurity.io/github/app/onebeyond/guidesmiths/repositories)
    - [Dashboard for OneBeyond Org](https://app.stepsecurity.io/github/app/onebeyond/onebeyond/repositories)
- We now have a new [newsletter generator](https://github.com/onebeyond/newsletter-generator) that will be utilized to gather all the metrics and events for this newsletter.
- We have implemented a new metric system using [Cauldron, developed by Bitergia](https://cauldron.io/project/7192?from_date=2023-01-01&to_date=2023-04-25&tab=overview&page=1). This will enable us to gain a better understanding of our organization through [advanced metrics](https://cauldron.io/project/7192?from_date=2022-04-25&to_date=2023-04-25&tab=chaoss) that follow the [CHAOSS recommendations](https://chaoss.community/).

**üôå Participate!**
- We would love to receive your feedback on the Scorecard StepSecurity Dashboard Beta. Kindly share your thoughts with us on Slack.
- We are spearheading an initiative to establish tiers for categorizing the Open Source projects within our organization. Please [join the discussion](https://github.com/onebeyond/maintainers/issues/75).
- Please join us for the upcoming maintainers meeting on May 18th, 2023!
