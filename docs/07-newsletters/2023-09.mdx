# September's Newsletter

## üì¢ Announcements and Updates

**üö® Important**

- We have started to homogenize the [Tier 1 projects](https://onebeyond-maintainers.netlify.app/governance/projects#tier-1-active) in terms of:
  - [Code Climate maintainability](https://github.com/onebeyond/maintainers/issues/127)
  - [Code Climate test coverage](https://github.com/onebeyond/maintainers/issues/131)
  - [Badges](https://github.com/onebeyond/maintainers/issues/129)

- We have also started to terraform our GtHub organization infrastructure. Take a look at [this repository](https://github.com/onebeyond/org-infra) if you want to learn how to do it!

**üì∞ News**

- October is coming, and with it a new edition of [Hacktoberfest](https://hacktoberfest.com/)! This year they are celebrating their 10th anniversary supporting Open Source! You'll be able to register from the 26th of September.
As you all know, in One Beyond :one-beyond-bw: we try to be an active member in the Open Source ecosystem and we have an Open Source community in GitHub. There you can find a lot of repositories where you can contribute and achieve your digital reward. There are tons of options to help and also learn new stuff:

  - Learn how to terraform the complete infrastructure of a GitHub organization
  - Contribute to our well-known [license-checker](https://github.com/onebeyond/license-checker)
  - Learn and help to release our [first OB GitHub action](https://github.com/onebeyond/license-checker-github-action) so we can use our license-checker from our CI/CD pipelines
  - Improve the notifications we send to slack by adding some new cool stuff to our [bamboohrbot](https://github.com/onebeyond/bamboohrbot)
  - Give some love to our beloved [systemic](https://github.com/onebeyond/systemic)
  - Learn about [Code Climate](https://codeclimate.com/) by integrating it in our repos
  - And much more... Ask the maintainers or in our slack channel if you want to know more!

**üôå Participate!**
- **You can sign the [One Beyond Open Source Manifesto](/open-source-manifesto) to show your support**.
- We want to increase the maintainers team. If you are interested please contact us on Slack.
- There are [issues](https://github.com/search?q=user%3Aonebeyond+user%3Aguidesmiths+label%3A%22help+wanted%22+state%3Aopen&type=Issues&ref=advsearch&l=&l=) with the `help wanted` tag. Please feel free to participate.
- Please join us for the upcoming maintainers meeting on Oct 5th, 2023!
